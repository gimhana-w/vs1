About VS One

Sri Lanka's Fastest Growing Value-Added IT Distributor

Founded in 2019

Starting with a vision to transform IT distribution in Sri Lanka, we've grown to become a trusted partner for businesses nationwide.

200+ Partners

Our growing island-wide partner network includes IT resellers, retailers, system integrators, and e-commerce marketplaces across Sri Lanka.

One-Stop Destination

From PCs and components to networking equipment, data-center solutions, and smart office systems - we cover all your IT needs.

Our Mission

VS One is committed to building a technology-enabled digital infrastructure system across Sri Lanka. We aim to bring global brands and top-quality services to customers even in remote areas, enabling digital access nationwide.

We emphasize long-term relationships with partners, reliability, integrity, and commitment to excellence in every aspect of our operations.

What We Offer

  • Comprehensive IT product distribution
  • Technical consultancy and support
  • Logistics and supply management
  • Warranty and after-sales service
  • Financial and credit schemes for partners
  • Pre-sales support and business development
